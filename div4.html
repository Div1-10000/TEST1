CEO OF TMA

Founded in 2020, TMA (Tarun Mobile Accessories) has grown into a trusted name in mobile solutions and accessories. Under the leadership of Mr. Tarun Sharma, CEO of TMA, the company has built a strong reputation for providing high-quality mobile accessories, expert repair services, and cutting-edge solutions for all types of smartphones. At TMA, we believe in delivering innovation, reliability, and customer satisfaction with every product and service. From premium chargers and earphones to advanced mobile maintenance and unlocking services, TMA continues to set new standards in the mobile accessories industry. Driven by passion and powered by technology, TMA is committed to keeping you connected—today and into the future.
